* ½ cup soy sauce
* ½ cup rice vinegar
* 2 tbsp rice wine
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 tbsp chili paste
* 1 cup water
* 3 tbsp cornstarch
* 1 tsp sesame oil

1. Combine everything but sesame oil in pan and stir until no lumps.
1. Heat on medium high until the sauce simmers and is thickened.
1. Remove from heat and stir in sesame oil.
